Katia Pase

I am an editor and I mainly make magazines and journals and books. Currently, I edit Australia's premier graphic design magazine desktop, and I am the co-editor of one of Australia's longest-running literary journals Going Down Swinging.

I also give occasional letures and I teach in the Department of Culture and Communication at the University of Melbourne.

Here you can find some of my work. It is also the work of my collaborators: designers, photographers, authors and other editors.
